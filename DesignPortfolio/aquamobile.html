AquaMobile is a platform used for booking private swimming lessons in which the swimming instructors come to you. The mobile app used by swimming instructors was not optimized to provide trouble free user experience.

Users

Users are swimming instructors, many of whom drive to their client locations. The app is frequently used throughout the day to check and update information.

Frustrations:
  • Navigation is not optimized.
  • Wrong status completions.
  • No way to get client addresses without copying and pasting.
Goals:
  • An overall more intuitive and optimized app.

User navigation

The most used section of the app was the “My Bookings” section and the second most used was the “My Availability” section. Neither were included in the bottom navigation.

Status completions

Instructors are required to click “Arrived” once they have arrived at the clients' location and click “Completed” once they have completed the lesson. Displaying bookings from non selected days and with minimal spacing resulted in instructors clicking “Arrived” and “Completed” for bookings on a different day.

Tertiary buttons

Instructors had no way to message and get directions without copying and pasting client information to different apps.

Contrast

Color scheme did not meet contrast standards.

Call to action

Call to action buttons are hard to identify due to the mixture of other contents with the same color scheme. Makes the process of editing availability and services confusing.
