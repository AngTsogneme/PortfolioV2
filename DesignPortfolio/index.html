About me

After learning to code, I realized I love problem solving and thinking innovatively. This fueled my desire to become a UX Designer because it allowed me to utilize my logic and creativity.

I realized just how challenging it is to create well designed products after learning the basics and creating projects on my own. The failures along the way would ultimately narrow my design philosphy to prioritize balance.

I think what sets apart a good design from the rest, are the result of how effectively the design balances simplicity with sophistication, usability with functionality, and user needs with business needs.
